# CyclopsRD — 5-Minute Pitch Rehearsal Script

## Introduction (0:00 - 0:45)
- **Problem Statement:** "Trillions are spent on roads, but we don't know *why* specific roads get fixed while others crumble."
- **The Hook:** "CyclopsRD (InfraLens) is the first 'audit engine' for infrastructure bias. We don't just map potholes; we map the decisions behind them."
- **The Tech:** "We use OpenStreetMap and graph theory to calculate an objective 'Engineering Priority' and compare it to real-world spending."

## The Demo - "The Map" (0:45 - 2:00)
- **Step 1: The Engineering Lens.** "Look at the red lines. These aren't just roads; they are the most critical nodes in Jambi's economic network, calculated via betweenness centrality and hierarchy."
- **Step 2: The Allocation Lens.** "Now, toggle the district overlay. Blue districts are under-resourced; red are over-resourced relative to their engineering need."

## The "Wow" Moment - "The Audit" (2:00 - 3:30)
- **Step 3: The Smoking Gun.** "Click on Jl. Sultan Thaha. It's Rank #2 in engineering priority but only Rank #14 in spending. That's a 12-rank transparency gap."
- **Step 4: The Explanation.** "Our AI explains why: it's a primary artery with high centrality serving 90k vehicles, yet it's being deprioritized. We've just flagged a potential governance anomaly."

## The Vision & Moat (3:30 - 4:30)
- **The Moat:** "We aren't just a tool; we are building the largest dataset of infrastructure decision patterns globally."
- **The Roadmap:** "From a Jambi prototype to the 'Bloomberg for Infrastructure Governance' that NGOs and banks use to audit trillions in lending."

## Conclusion & Q&A (4:30 - 5:00)
- **Closing:** "We bring transparency to infrastructure spending. One road at a time. Thank you."
